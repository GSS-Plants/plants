User Case

Julia Stone has a new plant and would like help remembering to water it.

Interaction Flow

  1. Julia logs on to plant.r
  2. plant.r opens home screen
  3. Julia types in the name of her plant in the search bar
  4. plant.r load her search results
  5. Julia selects the correct results
  6. plant.r loads her selection
  7. Julia reads over the information on the site
  8. Julia chooses to receive reminders
  9. plant.r thanks her and reminds her to be aware of her climate
  10. Julia takes into account her location
  11. Julia decides to water her plant every three days
  12. Julia closes the app
  13. plant.r reminds Julia to water plant three days later via email
  14. Julia waters her plant
