User Case

Edward wants to get more details about his collection of plants

Interaction Flow

  1. Edward logs on plant.r
  2. plant.r loads Edward's list of his saved plants
  3. Edward clicks on a plant that He wants information about
  4. plant.r shows Edward a summary of that plant
  5. Edward expands the summary that shows more details
  6. Edward reads about the plants soil pH growing season and toxicity
  7. Edward emails all of these fascinating details to himself
