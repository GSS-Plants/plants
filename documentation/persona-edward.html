User Persona

Edward: Plant Forgetter & registered user

User Story

"As a busy user, I want to use technology to help me keep my house plants alive."

Details

Edward likes to have plants around the house because it reminds him of growing up in Costa Rica. He is bad at remembering when to water them and how much, and has had to throw out an embarrassing number of dead, dried up house plants. He has a busy schedule and is very active on social media, so he almost always has his phone on him.

Technology

  • Devices: Has a Macbook Pro for work, got an iPhone 11 and 8th gen iPad for personal use to avoid learning a new OS.
  • Browser: Safari across all devices.
  • Comfort: Highly proficient with all of his devices.
  • Attitude: Likes to be a power-user with technology that saves him time.

Frustrations

  • Not knowing how to take care of his plants.
  • Having to throw out plants he let die.
  • Things that can't be automated.
  • Getting plants as gifts with no instructions for taking care of them.

Needs

  • Edward needs regular reminders to water his plants.
  • The reminders should tell him which plant needs water, and how much.
  • Edward needs his houseplants to add peace and comfort to his life, not complexity.

Goals

Edward's goal is to keep a wide variety of houseplants and keep them healthy with as little frustration as possible. He wants a service that will let him look up how to care for his plants and remind him what they need and when.
